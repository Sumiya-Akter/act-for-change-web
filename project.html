Climate Initiatives

United Nations Climate Change


The UN works on various projects all over the world to aid in the issue of climate change.

Some of the topics they've breached are carbon emissions, deforestation, and solar projections.

Visit their page to see all of their projects on display. Maybe you can get inspiration from them to do something on your own!

USAID Partnership



The USAID Partnership aims to do the same thing that the UN is doing, but a little more focused on energy.

Some of the things that they have been working on are:

  • Renewable energy integration
  • grid modernization
  • distributed energy resources and storage
  • energy efficiency
  • power sector resilience
  • data collection and analysis

To learn more and track their progress, be sure to give their website a visit!

Servir Global

SERVIR is improving awareness, increasing access to information, and supporting analysis to help people.

Their help reaches people in West Africa, Eastern and Southern Africa, Hindu Kush-Himalaya, the Lower Mekong, South America and Mesoamerica.

This allows the people in those locations to be able to manage challenges in the areas of food security, water resources, land use change, and natural disasters.

To learn more about their cause and how to help, make sure to visit their website!
